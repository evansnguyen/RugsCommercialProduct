ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Our beginnings are humble. Frm two looms in India to be the trusted rugs designer in Australia. Every step of our journey has been taken with beauty and community in mind. We create and sell luxurious, elgant prducts that bring the world together.

information

Persian Living is a family business establish by a daring son Indi Rujuk, whose dream is to bring his farther, Malak Rujuk's skill of craftmanship to the world.

Our purpose is to create beautiful lives for our customers, consumers, their families and friends, the weavers who effort were contributed the most, and our employees.

We are committed to the craftsmanship and artfulness of handmade rus, and to keeping the age-old art alive.

GIVING BACK

Persian Living are committed to giving back to the communities in India that make an invaluable contribution to our business. Most of the weavers are woman who to us, are also a mother, a sister, a family member that we are fully trusted in.

information

Persian Living has contribute our profit to establish The Alternative Education Program with the aim of introducing literacy, numeracy and life skills into selected rural areas where weaving takes place. Today, more than 2500 women and children have been educated through the program.
